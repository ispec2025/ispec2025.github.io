ISPEC 2025

20th International Conference on Information Security Practice and Experience

Chengdu, China 7-9 November 2025

Call-for-Paper

ISPEC 2025 will be held in Chengdu, China, organized by University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, China. The conference seeks submissions presenting novel contributions related to information and communication security.
